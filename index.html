Effective Date: April 30, 2026 This Privacy Policy applies to the Slash Pop mobile application on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). 1. Introduction This Privacy Policy describes how Hest Studios ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Slash Pop mobile application (the "App") on iOS and Android devices. By downloading or using Slash Pop, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App. 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Information You Provide Slash Pop does not require account creation, registration, or login. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier. 2.2 Information Collected Automatically When you use the App, certain information is automatically collected through third-party services. This includes: Device identifiers — including the Advertising Identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android), used by ad networks for personalized advertising and frequency capping. Approximate location — derived from your IP address (country/region level only). We do not access GPS or precise location. Device and technical information — operating system, device model, screen resolution, app version, language, time zone. App activity and usage — game events such as session start, session end, level reached, score, in-game purchases (none currently), and ad interactions. Diagnostic data — performance metrics and error reports used to diagnose crashes and improve stability. 2.3 Information Stored Locally on Your Device The following data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party: Personal best score, total coins, daily streak count. Selected trail skin and unlocked skin progress. Daily quest progress and rewards claimed. Audio and notification preferences. This data remains on your device. It is removed if you uninstall the App or clear app data. 3. Third-Party Services Slash Pop integrates the following third-party SDKs that may collect data as described above. Each provider has its own privacy practices, which we encourage you to review: 3.1 Google AdMob (Advertising) We display advertisements through Google AdMob to support the free version of the App. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data to serve personalized ads. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy and AdMob Privacy Information. 3.2 Google Firebase Analytics (Analytics) We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how players use the App, identify popular features, and improve gameplay. Firebase collects app-event data and a Firebase installation ID. Learn more: Firebase Privacy and Security. 3.3 Apple Game Center (iOS only) On iOS devices, leaderboard scores may be submitted to Apple Game Center if you have Game Center enabled on your device. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. 4. How We Use the Information To operate, maintain, and improve the App. To display advertisements and measure ad performance. To analyze gameplay patterns and identify bugs or performance issues. To comply with legal obligations. We do not sell personal data to third parties. 5. Advertisements Slash Pop is supported by advertising. Two types of ads are shown: Interstitial ads are displayed automatically between gameplay sessions (approximately every 3 plays). Rewarded ads are entirely optional — you may choose to watch one to earn an extra life or bonus coins. They only appear if you tap the reward button. 6. Children's Privacy Slash Pop is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App's content rating on the App Store and Google Play reflects this audience targeting. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can remove it. 7. Your Rights and Choices 7.1 Limit Ad Tracking You can limit advertising tracking on your device: iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" or, on first launch, choose "Ask App Not to Track". Android: Settings → Google → Ads → enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or reset Advertising ID. 7.2 Delete Local Data You can delete all locally stored game data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data through your device settings. 7.3 GDPR / CCPA Rights If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete data and to object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. 8. Data Retention Data collected by AdMob and Firebase Analytics is retained according to those services' own retention policies. We do not maintain our own user database or backend server. Locally stored game data persists on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data. 9. Data Security All data transmitted by the App to third-party services (AdMob, Firebase) is encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. 10. International Data Transfers The third-party services we use (Google AdMob, Firebase, Apple Game Center) may transfer and process data in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. These transfers are subject to the privacy practices of each provider. 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top will reflect the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. 12.
