Privacy Policy

Slash Pop by heststudios
Last updated: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how heststudios ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the mobile game Slash Pop ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Slash Pop is designed to be enjoyed without the need to create an account or share personal information. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Your location
  • Your photos, videos, or contacts
  • Any other personally identifiable information you voluntarily provide

However, the following information may be collected automatically through third-party services we use:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, language, region
  • Game analytics: Session duration, levels played, in-game progress, crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers: A device-specific identifier used for serving and measuring ads

2. How Your Information Is Used

The information collected automatically is used to:

  • Deliver in-app advertisements through our ad partners
  • Measure user engagement and improve the gameplay experience
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Slash Pop uses the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — Used to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to deliver personalized or contextual advertising.
    Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase Analytics — Used to understand how players engage with the App and to identify crashes.
    Privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Apple App Store — When the App is installed through the App Store, Apple may collect data in accordance with its own policies.
    Privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Advertising

Slash Pop displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These advertisements may use device identifiers to deliver relevant content. On iOS 14.5+, Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework requires us to ask for your permission before tracking your activity across other apps and websites. You may decline this permission at any time, and the App will continue to function. You may also reset your device's advertising identifier at any time through your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Slash Pop does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App is rated 4+ and is intended for general audiences. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Data Security

We do not store user data on our own servers. Any data collected by third-party services is handled in accordance with their own security practices. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information transmitted, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., the European Union under GDPR or California under CCPA), you may have the right to:

  • Access information collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent for tracking-based advertising

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below. Note that since we do not directly collect personal information, most data requests will need to be directed to the third-party services listed above.
